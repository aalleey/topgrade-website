What is TopGradeSoftware?

TopGradeSoftware is an all-in-one school management system that helps manage administrative and academic activities such as online admission management, fees, employees' payroll, examination, grade book, bulk data management, transportation, library, course and batches, student attendance, assignments, communication and much more.

What are the pricing packages?

We offer three packages based on modules and student strength:
  • Silver: Rs 3,000 (up to 300 students)
  • Gold: Rs 5,000 (up to 800 students)
  • Diamond: Rs 10,000 (up to 2000 students)

Does TopGradeSoftware offer demo accounts?

Not at the moment, but we'll be providing demo sessions soon.

Is there a mobile application available?

Yes, we have mobile applications for parents, teachers, and students. The mobile app provides easy access to important features and notifications.

What features are available?

  • Student Profiles Management
  • Attendance Management
  • Branded SMS System
  • Fee Management & Fee Vouchers
  • Parents & Teachers Portal
  • Exams Management
  • Transport Management
  • Inventory/Stock Management
  • Expenses, Profit & Loss Reports
  • Payroll System
  • Mobile Application
  • Timetable Management
  • Datesheet & Roll No Slips
  • DLP & Syllabus Management
  • And many more...

What is the installation process?

The installation is done online. After collecting all necessary information, we perform the online installation and create your username and password. The deployment time is around 1 day, and data entry time depends upon your school's strength.

Does TopGradeSoftware offer support and training?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support and training to all our clients to ensure they get the most out of our software.
